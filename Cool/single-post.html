Our Valued Partners

We are proud to collaborate with these esteemed partners who share our commitment to excellence in the fashion, modeling, and makeup industries.

SOS Children Village: International NGOs.

The Fashion Industry Association, our primary partner, provides extensive resources and networking, significantly enhancing our students' opportunities for career growth within the fashion sector.

Finnish Refugee Council: International NGOs.

Global Makeup Brands, a leader in cosmetics, partners with us to offer advanced makeup workshops and exclusive product access, ensuring our students master the latest beauty techniques.

OPRIFS: National NGOs.

We are proud to collaborate with OPRIFS, a national NGO dedicated to preventing and protecting children, girls, and women from abuse and violence. Together, we are working towards a safer future.

Private Garment and Fashion industries.

Sustainable Textiles Group joins us in promoting eco-conscious fashion, offering educational programs and materials that emphasize ethical and sustainable design practices for our students.

Government offices.

Partnering with Retail Fashion Group opens doors for our students to internships and placements in leading retail environments, providing practical experience in merchandising and sales.

Private Charity Organizations and others.

Cool Fashion Academy collaborates with private charities, possibly offering skills in design, makeup, or modeling to empower vulnerable individuals they support.
