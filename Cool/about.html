Company History

Cool Fashion is a private profit-making business institution founded in 2011 E.C by designers who graduated from BahirDar University with degrees in Textile and Fashion Design and well-known training center in providing high-quality practical and theoretical training skills to the community in field of Fashion Design,Garment and Textile Industry in Addis.

The institution has more than 5 years training experience in equipping with many trainees train in the field by opening training institutes in various places according to the guidelines laid down in of technical and vocational education and training sector.

Founders of Cool fashion

The founder of Cool Fashion Design, Makeup, and Modeling Academy is a highly motivated and results-driven Textile Engineer (BSc). They possess over a decade of combined experience in quality assurance, management, design development, and standardization within the textile industry. Their professional journey includes five years at Augusta Cloth Factory, specializing in quality assurance and new design development. They also served over five years on the Technical Committee (TC70) at the Institute of Ethiopian Standards, contributing to industry benchmarks.

Currently, they offer consultancy in apparel manufacturing, focusing on quality inspection and business growth. Passionate about optimization, they empower businesses to achieve sustainable progress within the textile and apparel sector. As the founder of Cool Design Creativity House and Cool Fashion Academy, they lead a dynamic team. This entrepreneurial spirit fosters innovation and nurtures talent in fashion and design, reflecting their commitment to industry advancement.
